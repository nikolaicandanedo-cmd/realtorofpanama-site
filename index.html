About Us

Two Decades of Expertise in Panamanian Real Estate

Nikolai Candanedo began his real estate career in Panama City in 1997 and earned his professional license in 2002, building deep knowledge of Panama's evolving property market over five formative years in the field. In 2014 he relocated to Boquete, Chiriquí — drawn by the region's natural beauty and growing appeal among international buyers — and has since made it his permanent base of operations.

Today, with more than two decades of experience and a broad entrepreneurial background that spans real estate development, logistics franchising, and the local tourism industry, Nikolai serves both local residents and foreign investors looking to buy, sell, rent, or manage property throughout Panama City and the scenic Chiriquí highlands. His hands-on knowledge of off-market opportunities and direct relationships with property owners set him apart.

Proud ACOBIR member. Listings span the Boquete highlands, Volcán, Cerro Punta, and the broader Chiriquí region.
