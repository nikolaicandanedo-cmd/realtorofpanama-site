Privacy Policy

Realtor of Panama

Last updated: May 12, 2026

This policy explains how Realtor of Panama collects, uses, and protects information when you contact us by email, phone, WhatsApp, or through our website.

What we collect

When you contact us, we may collect your name, phone number, email address, the content of messages you send us, and information about the property or services you are interested in. If you message us on WhatsApp, we receive the information WhatsApp provides about your account, including your phone number and any messages, images, or files you send.

How we use information

We use the information you provide to respond to your inquiries, help you find or sell property, share listings that match your interests, and maintain a record of our business relationship. We do not sell your information to third parties.

WhatsApp messaging

Messages you send us on WhatsApp may be processed by automated systems so we can respond quickly. A person at Realtor of Panama reviews and answers messages that require personal attention. Your messages are stored so we can continue conversations across days or weeks and serve you well over time.

Your rights

You can ask us at any time what information we have about you, request corrections, or ask us to delete your information. To do so, email info@realtorofpanama.com with your request and we will respond within a reasonable time.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to info@realtorofpanama.com.
